Revision cost advantage
Manual re-records require the full process again. Synthesia edits are script-only — near-zero marginal cost per update.
Quality floor rises
Removes variability in audio quality, bad takes, and background noise — common pain points with Teams recordings.
Scale without headcount
Manual output is roughly linear with ID time. Synthesia breaks that — more videos don't require proportionally more hours.
Speed to learner
Same-day turnaround vs. days of scheduling, recording, and processing. Critical for fast-moving programs.
Sources: Talk track time derived from industry benchmark of 125–150 words/min spoken pace (Teleprompter.com, 2024); recording prep and re-take norms based on Teams screen-recording guidance (Marquette University IT, 2023; HYMS Learning Technologies, 2023); export/processing time from standard PowerPoint-to-video workflow documentation (Microsoft Support, 2024). Long-form agency cost baseline of $20,000 per video per eLearning Industry (2025). Synthesia process range per Synthesia platform documentation (7–30 min).
